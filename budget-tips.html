Know where your money goes!
       How much income do you have?
       How much are each of your bills?
       Make sure to budget for savings! Set a goal. This is where you think about the future!
       Do you want to pay down student loans?
       Buy a house?
       Take a trip?
       Save for retirement? Beware of bad advice.
       Research what complicated terms mean so you can develop your own ideas of what financial stability is.
       Know your options- there are so many ways to create financial security for yourself, but its important to understand things like compound interest, high yield security.
       YOLO- you only live once, so make it a financially comfortable one in the long run. A good baseline is 50% of your total income goes to needs, like loans, rent, and groceries. 30% of income goes to your ‘wants’; this is where you would dedicate funds to concert tickets, trips, a new pair of sweet kicks. And the final (and VERY important part) is 20% of your income should go to savings!
